Welcome to Sample Auto Services, your professional and reliable insurance broker in California. We provide personal auto insurance and specialize in auto registrations such as sticker or tags renewals, change of ownership of vehicles, lien sales, and much more.

Our services include:

  • California Vehicle Registration
  • Instant Renewals
  • California Vehicle Title Transfers
  • Vin Verification
  • Replacement Plates

To expedite the process of your renewals, please bring the following:

  • Smog Check
  • Proof of valid Insurance
  • Renewal Slip

For title transfers, please bring the following:

  • Pink slip.
  • (If it’s lost you can bring the registration or the renewal slip)
  • Smog Check
  • Proof of valid Insurance
  • Copy of ID
